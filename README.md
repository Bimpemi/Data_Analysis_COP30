# Data_Analysis_COP30
Data analysis on climate change event held in Brazil- COP30

Data analysis of Global North (GN) and Global South (GS) narratives of the COP30 event in 2025 held in Brazil.
The analysis identifies the percentage of Global South narratives in climate change over the Global North narratives; these narratives influence global decision-making on climate change. 
The analysis also identifies the Global North mainstream media as the top in creating awareness about climate change, sharing information about its narratives, and participation of GN and GS leaders in climate change meetings and events to the public.
